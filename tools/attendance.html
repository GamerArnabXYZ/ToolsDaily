Frequently asked questions

How do I use the attendance tracker?

Type a student's name and add them to the list. Tap the 'P' button to mark present or 'A' for absent; the present/absent counts and attendance rate update automatically.

Is the attendance data saved or uploaded?

No. Names and marks stay in your browser only — nothing is uploaded or stored on a server, so student data remains private.

Can I reset the attendance sheet?

Yes. Use the 'Clear all' button to empty the list and start a fresh roll call.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once the page is loaded, the attendance tracker works without an internet connection.

Can I track attendance for multiple classes?

Yes. Clear the list between periods or keep separate browser tabs open for each class — each tab keeps its own roll call.
