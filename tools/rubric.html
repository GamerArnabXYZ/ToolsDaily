Frequently asked questions

How does the rubric scorer work?

Add each criterion with its weight and the points earned, and the tool computes a normalized total out of 100.

What does 'weight' mean on a rubric?

Weight shows how much a criterion counts toward the final score — a weight of 2 counts twice as much as a weight of 1.

Can I add multiple criteria?

Yes. Use 'Add criterion' for as many rows as your rubric needs.

Is my data uploaded?

No. All scoring happens locally in your browser.
