Frequently asked questions

How do I convert HEX to RGB?

Select a color with the picker or type a HEX value, and the RGB and HSL equivalents appear automatically for you to copy.

What color formats are supported?

The tool converts between HEX, RGB, and HSL — the three most common formats for web and design work.

Can I copy each color value?

Yes. Each format has its own copy button so you can grab HEX, RGB, or HSL individually.

Is the color picker free?

Yes, it runs entirely in your browser with no uploads or sign-up.

Can I use this to match a color from a design file?

Yes. Pick the closest shade on the color wheel or type a known HEX/RGB value to fine-tune it, then copy the format you need.
