Frequently asked questions

What sizes are generated?

16×16 and 32×32 for browser tabs, 180×180 for Apple touch icon, and 192×192 and 512×512 for Android home screen and PWA icons.

Does my image need to be square?

Square images work best since every size is a square. Non-square images will be stretched to fit — crop to a square first for best results.

Why is there no .ico file?

Modern browsers fully support PNG favicons, so a legacy .ico file isn't required. Just add the generated PNGs and the HTML snippet below.

Is my image uploaded anywhere?

No. All resizing happens locally in your browser using Canvas — the image never leaves your device.

Where do I put the downloaded files?

Place them in your website's root folder (or wherever you keep static assets) and paste the provided HTML snippet into your page's <head>.
