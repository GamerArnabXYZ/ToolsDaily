Frequently asked questions

How do I create a quiz?

Add questions, type the correct answer and a wrong option for each, then press 'Start quiz' to let someone answer inline.

Does it score the quiz automatically?

Yes. After each answer the tool marks it right or wrong and shows the final correct count when finished.

Is the quiz saved anywhere?

No. Questions and answers stay in your browser and are not uploaded or stored.

Can I use this in class?

Yes. It is designed for quick, low-prep classroom checks with no login required.
