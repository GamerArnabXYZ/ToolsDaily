Frequently asked questions

How is age calculated exactly?

The tool counts complete years, then complete months, then remaining days between your birth date and the reference date — the same way age is counted on official documents.

Can I calculate the gap between two dates that aren't a birthday?

Yes. Set "Calculate age as of" to any date instead of today, and set "Birth date" to your start date — the result shows the exact gap between them.

Does it account for leap years?

Yes. The calculation uses actual calendar dates, so leap years are handled automatically and don't cause any drift.

Why does it show days until my next birthday?

It's a small bonus — a quick countdown to your next birthday based on the reference date you selected.

Is my birth date stored or sent anywhere?

No. The calculation happens entirely in your browser and nothing is saved or transmitted.
