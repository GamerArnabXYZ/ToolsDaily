Frequently asked questions

How secure are the generated passwords?

Passwords are created with the browser's cryptographic random number generator (crypto.getRandomValues), which is suitable for security use.

What makes a password strong?

Length and variety: more characters and a mix of upper, lower, numbers, and symbols raise entropy, shown as 'bits' in the tool.

Is my password stored or sent anywhere?

No. Generation happens entirely on your device and nothing is saved or uploaded.

How long should my password be?

Aim for at least 12–16 characters; longer is stronger. Use the length slider to increase it.

Are the passwords really random?

Yes. Passwords are generated using your browser's cryptographically secure random number generator, not a predictable pattern.
