Frequently asked questions

How are grades calculated?

Each row's score is multiplied by its weight, the weighted totals are summed, and divided by the total weight to produce your final grade.

What does the weight mean?

Weight is the percentage a task contributes to the final grade — for example, a midterm worth 30% gets a weight of 30.

Can I add more than a few assignments?

Yes. Use 'Add row' as many times as you need to include every graded item.

Is my data stored?

No. All calculations happen in your browser and nothing is saved or uploaded.

What if my weights don't add up to 100%?

The calculator normalizes weights automatically, so your final grade is still accurate even if the weights total more or less than 100%.
