Frequently asked questions

Which unit categories are supported?

The converter covers common categories such as length, mass (weight), and temperature, with more selectable from the dropdown.

How do I convert between units?

Pick a category, choose the from and to units, type an amount, and the result appears instantly.

Is the conversion accurate?

Yes. Conversions use standard factors; temperature uses the correct formula rather than a simple ratio.

Is my input uploaded?

No. All conversions happen locally in your browser.

How accurate are the conversions?

Conversions use standard, precise conversion factors, so results are accurate enough for schoolwork, cooking, and everyday use.
