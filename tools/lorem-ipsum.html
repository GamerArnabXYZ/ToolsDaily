Frequently asked questions

What is Lorem Ipsum used for?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text used by designers and developers to fill layouts before real content is ready, so the focus stays on structure and design.

Is the text actually Latin?

It's based on a scrambled passage of Latin text from a 1st-century BC work by Cicero, though it isn't meant to be readable or translated.

Can I generate just a few words instead of paragraphs?

Yes. Switch to "Words" and set the count to get exactly the number of words you need.

Does it always start with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet"?

By default, yes — that's the traditional opening. You can uncheck the option to get fully randomized placeholder text instead.

Is there a limit to how much I can generate?

You can generate up to 50 paragraphs, sentences, or words at a time — enough for most mockups and layouts.
