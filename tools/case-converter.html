Frequently asked questions

What case styles are supported?

UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, camelCase, snake_case, and kebab-case are all supported in one tap.

What's the difference between camelCase and snake_case?

camelCase joins words with no spaces and capitalizes each word after the first (myVariableName). snake_case joins words with underscores in lowercase (my_variable_name).

Is my text uploaded anywhere?

No. Conversion happens locally in your browser and your text never leaves your device.

Does it work with multiple sentences or paragraphs?

Yes. Sentence case and Title Case handle multiple sentences and line breaks correctly.

Can I use this for code variable names?

Yes. camelCase and snake_case are commonly used for variable and function naming across programming languages.
