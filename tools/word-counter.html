Frequently asked questions

What does the word counter measure?

It counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and estimates reading time as you type.

Is my text uploaded?

No. Counting happens locally in your browser, so your writing stays private.

Does it update in real time?

Yes. Stats update instantly as you type or paste text into the box.

Why is reading time useful?

It helps you gauge how long a piece will take to read — handy for essays, articles, and social posts.

Does it count words in languages other than English?

Yes. Word and character counting works for any language using standard spacing and punctuation, not just English.
