Frequently asked questions

      

        Which citation styles are supported?
        
The citation builder generates references in APA, MLA, and Chicago styles. Pick a style and the format adjusts automatically.

      

      

        What information do I need to enter?
        
Typically the author, title, year, and publisher or site. Adding a URL is optional but recommended for web sources.

      

      

        Is the generated citation guaranteed correct?
        
The tool formats your input consistently, but always double-check against your institution's style guide, as edge cases can vary.

      

      

        Do I need an account to use it?
        
No. The citation builder is free and runs in your browser with no sign-up.
