Privacy Policy

Last updated:

The short version

This is a static website. Every tool runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not collect, upload, store, or transmit the content you enter. When you format JSON, count words, or generate a password, that data stays on your device.

What we don't collect

  • The text, files, or inputs you process with any tool.
  • Account information — there are no accounts to create.
  • Payment details — all tools are free.

Analytics & cookies

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand which tools are popular and improve the site. Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser and collects information such as pages visited, device type, and approximate location. This data is processed by Google under its own Privacy Policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by blocking cookies in your browser settings. If we ever add advertising or additional analytics providers, we will update this page first.

Third-party services

We use Google Analytics (see above) and may load web fonts from Google Fonts. These send standard requests to Google's servers but do not include the content you process inside our tools — all tool inputs (text, passwords, JSON, images, etc.) stay in your browser and are never transmitted to us or to these third parties.

Children's privacy

Daily Tools is safe for classroom use. Because we collect no personal data from tool usage, there is nothing to associate with a child user. Teachers and parents can use the tools with confidence.

Your choices

You can clear your browser's local storage at any time to remove any preferences saved on your device. Because we retain no server-side data about you, there is nothing for us to delete.
