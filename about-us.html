About

Small tools, built to stay out of your way.

Daily Tools started with a simple frustration: every time I needed to count words, format JSON, or check a password, the "free" tool I opened buried the input behind a sign-up wall, three pop-ups, and a page that took forever to load. So I built my own — small, fast, no-nonsense utilities that just work.

What we believe

  • Instant. Pages load fast and tools run as you type — no waiting on a server.
  • Private. Your text never leaves your browser. Hashing, formatting and counting all happen on-device.
  • Free, forever. No accounts, no paywalls, no feature teasers.
  • Plain. Vanilla HTML, CSS and JavaScript. No frameworks, no trackers, no bloat.

Who it's for

Students drafting essays, teachers building lesson timers, and developers tidying JSON — anyone who reaches for a utility a few times a day and doesn't want a fuss.
